Hunt The Wumpus!

Team "Byte Me"
Aaron Ong, Chris Johnston, Nadia Medvinsky, Priya Nakkiran

Keyboard Controls:

A, W, D - Left, Forward, Right

E - Fire Arrow

B - Buy Menu

Escape - Quit

Numbers 1-4 can be used in Buy menu and trivia pop-ups.

XBOX 360 Controls:
Yes, you can plug in a wired XBOX 360 controller!

Left Analog Stick - Movement

Right Trigger - Fire Arrow

Left Bumper - Buy Menu

Start - Start/Quit

Face buttons (A, B, X, Y) are used in all pop-ups, and correspond to the matching colors.

Touchscreen/Mouse controls also work, however a keyboard is needed for entering a custom seed and player name.

How to play

Navigate through the cave in order to fight the Wumpus. The only way you can kill the Wumpus is by shooting it with an arrow through an open doorway. If you run out of arrows or die, you will lose.
